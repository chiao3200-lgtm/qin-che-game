THE PEAK OF VICTORY
遊戲規則

• 每局隨機決定玩家或秦徹先手。
• 卡牌將不重複輪替出現，共 6 組目標。
• 率先湊齊系統指定「目標牌組」即獲勝。
• 連續輸給秦徹 5 回，將觸發「懲罰遊戲」。
• 特定卡牌可解鎖專屬對話與神祕相簿。

GAME RULES

• Turn order is randomly decided each round.
• Targets rotate through 6 unique sets without repetition.
• First to match the specified "Target" wins the round.
• Losing 5 times in a row triggers the "Punishment Game".
• Specific cards unlock unique stories and secret albums.
